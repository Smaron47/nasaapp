About Cosmix is a user-friendly app that gathers open science data from different places on the internet, like federal agencies and trusted sources, and puts it all in one convenient platform. This way, it allows users to access and make use of these data resources without the hassle of searching all over the web. Additionally, the app lets people come together as a community to explore, talk about, and work together on projects relevant to their topic interests through mapping data for societal benefits
